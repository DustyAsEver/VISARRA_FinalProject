About UrbanTreads

Our Story

Founded in 2020, UrbanTreads was born out of a passion for sneaker culture and a desire to bring authenticity to the reselling market. We started as a small group of enthusiasts who wanted to connect collectors with premium, verified sneakers. Today, we’re proud to be a trusted name in the sneaker community, offering a curated selection of limited-edition and high-demand kicks.

Our mission is simple: to provide sneakerheads with a seamless shopping experience while ensuring every pair is 100% authentic. From rare drops to timeless classics, we’ve got your feet covered.
